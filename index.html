About Me

I grew up in Nigeria but spent most of my education in England, at an all girls boarding school, and further I am originally from India.

This amalgamation of culture has made me intellectually curious. Therefore when I discovered the world of programming I found a space where there is constant advancement and ever more to be learned.

I graduated from the University of Manchester two years ago and have since worked in various industries: From the challenging but extremely rewarding areas of fashion and events to teaching Spanish in an Italian International School where I got to use and hone my Spanish skills.

I discovered coding a year ago and fell in love. Starting out as an enthusiastic bedroom web developer I was able to make a simple website for a manufacturing company (Olympic Inks Limited) after which I decided to pursue a fulltime course in web development in order to learn as much coding as possible.

Having just completed a Web Development Intensive course at General Assembly I am keen to advance my skills as a professional in a full stack junior development role.
